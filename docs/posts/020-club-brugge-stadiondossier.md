# Post 020: Club Brugge Stadiondossier

## Status

Ready to publish.

## Source Hook

Club Brugge announced on July 9, 2026 that the Raad voor Vergunningsbetwistingen rejected the appeal against the environmental permit for the new stadium at the Olympiasite. Club says it now has a definitive and executable permit after more than twenty years, expects construction to start this year, and expects to begin the 2028/2029 season in the new stadium.

## Why This Works

The joke treats the stadium dossier as if it has been part of Bruges civic life for so long that finally building the stadium feels like releasing an administrative artifact into the wild.
