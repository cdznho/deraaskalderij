# Post 019: De Wever Secret Santa

## Status

Ready to publish.

## Source Hook

HLN listed a story among its most-read items on July 9, 2026: Bart De Wever reportedly received a revolver as a gift from Erdogan at the NATO summit and only discovered it in Melsbroek.

## Why This Works

The joke treats a diplomatic weapon gift as an overenthusiastic Secret Santa exchange. The punchline is not the weapon itself, but the Belgian administrative reflex around goodiebags, scanners, and forms.

MELSBROEK - Na de ontdekking van een wel erg stevig relatiegeschenk in de bagage van Bart De Wever wil Melsbroek voortaan elke NAVO-goodiebag preventief door de scanner halen.
